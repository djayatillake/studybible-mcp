On Sources

Scripture is the Berean Standard Bible (BSB), dedicated to the public domain. Hebrew and Greek text, lexical data (BDB / LSJ / Strong's), study notes and the Bible dictionary (Tyndale), cross-references (Treasury of Scripture Knowledge and curated sets), Ancient-Near-East context, divine-council / two-powers readings (Michael Heiser), and the Moshe Kline "Woven Torah" pairings were drawn from the Study Bible tools. Two-powers and divine-council notes reflect one school of reading; alternatives are noted in place.
